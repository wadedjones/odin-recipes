Salsa Recipe

Salsa Description

This is my famous salsa recipe that I definitely got from the internet and nobody else has eaten.

  • Can of tomatoes
  • Can of Rotel
  • Half a white onion
  • One whole jalepeno
  • One handful of cilantro
  • The juice from one fresh squeezed lime
  • A pinch garlic powder
  • A pinch of cumin
  • A pinch of chili powder
  • Salt and peper to taste

Steps for the Salsa creation

  1. The steps are easy.
  2. Throw everything in a blender.
  3. Hit the blend button.
